wingnut Is Being Repaved and Extricated

The Construction Structure and Content of this site is being repaved by customization of material scavenged from nfoCentrale for the making wingnut independent of Orcmid's Lair.  Repaving includes repair of defects introduced in web server migrations and, now, modification for extrication and independent preservation in GitHub Pages.  At that point, these become legacy pages no longer maintained under a FrontPage/IIS/VSS Site Builder model.

The extricated Wingnut Sandbox provides for questions and answers at the Discussion Section.  Improvements and removal of defects in these preserved legacy materials can be reported and addressed in the Issues Section.  There are also relevant Projects from time to time.

 

For further information, refer to

  • c000001: wingnut Construction Materials Catalog

The catalog provides status and links to all nfoCentrale construction artifacts, including the available template materials.

Immediate Content

This section holds the 00-block of template materials applicable in the construction of wingnut web pages and construction notes.  Templates and other special plumbing materials are provided in folios having identifiers of form c0000xx.  These are allocated in blocks for maintenance purposes. 

The blocks also provide a correspondence in depth from the wingnut front porch that also matches the level of accession folders.  E.g., wingnut construction/templates/00/ matches the depth of  wingnut construction/2006/01/, facilitating use of the templates for creation of content materials.

There are presently no folders below templates/00.  Any templates/10/ folder commences with c000010, etc.

This construction section is part of the engineering, construction management, maintenance, and infrastructure for wingnut.  You are welcome to explore this and other construction-information pages.   You'll need your hard hat and safety shoes.
