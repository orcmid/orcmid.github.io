wingnut Is Being Repaved and Extricated

The Construction Structure and Content of this site is being repaved by customization of material scavenged from nfoCentrale for the making wingnut independent of Orcmid's Lair.  Repaving includes repair of defects introduced in web server migrations and, now, modification for extrication and independent preservation in GitHub Pages.  At that point, these become legacy pages no longer maintained under a FrontPage/IIS/VSS Site Builder model.

The extricated Wingnut Sandbox provides for questions and answers at the Discussion Section.  Improvements and removal of defects in these preserved legacy materials can be reported and addressed in the Issues Section.  There are also relevant Projects from time to time.

 
 

This section holds template-material subfolders.  The templates are applicable to the construction of wingnut aweb pages and construction notes.

For further information, refer to the low-numbered Template entries in

  • c000001: wingnut Construction Materials Catalog

The catalog provides status and links to all wingnut construction artifacts, including the available template materials.

Immediate Structure

00/

Initial block of templates.  The number on the folder determines the lowest-numbered material.  The numbers c000000 through c000099 are reserved for plumbing and templates.   The blocks tend to be in 10s in order to simplify folder maintenance.  E.g., c000010 would begin folder 10/
