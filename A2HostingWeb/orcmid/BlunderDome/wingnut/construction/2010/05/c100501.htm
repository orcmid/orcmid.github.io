The original repaving of the nfoCentrale anchor site construction materials is adapted to the repaving of the orcmid/BlunderDome/wingnut transposition of that material.  The touch-up of existing wingnut materials will be transposed to this repaving project as part of exfiltration preparations.  This involves using FrontPage as long as possible before the legacy content is moved to GitHub pages where such editing is no longer available.  Although there could continue to be maintenance under the nfoCentrale structure, that will not be possible as web-site domains and the computers used for the FrontPage, IIS, and VSS SiteBuilder authoring approach are retired.

1. Bootstrapping

Bootstrapping of Wingnut Sandbox involves creating an Orcmid's Lair folder with the minimal structure that is used for all nfoCentrale sites, even though it does not have its own domain there.  The minimal structure provides scaffolding on which the wingnut portion can be exfiltrated into GitHub pages as a project of its own.  This perpetuates the use of Wingnut as a laboratory in further framework experiments and also preserves its historical employment.

  The basic infrastructure of the site is arranged first:

    wingnut / ---   root construction structure and root construction zone
                |
   
                |
                -- mt-static/
                |
                -- construction/
                |
                -- images/
 

and other subfolder introduced on behalf of additional frameworks employed for experimental purposes.  There is already content being preserved as well.

Transposition for exfiltration is a manual process.  In order to keep track of the status and progress, notices are placed throughout the construction structure and in lists of work items (e.g., on any Job Jar & Diary page) that reflect the local transposition (and later-maintenance) status. 

The customization process begins at the front porch of the site.  It is propagated from there down into the infrastructure and content sections as they are introduced.  The complete transformation progress of all components of the site can be reviewed by navigation from the root construction structure page.

When transposition is confirmed by review in-place at Orcmid's Liar, the exfiltration to GitHub Pages at the Wingnut Sandbox can be carried out.

2. Work Items and Progress

There is a progression of work items being followed to extend the construction structure information through all of the initial folders.  This can be found in the top-level construction zone Diary & Job Jar table. 

Essentially, the initial material is adjusted starting from the root of the site and working to the leaves of the initial folders.  As the material is customized, notices are inserted in the pages of infrastructure to reflect their bootstrapping status.  

The initial notice is in red text.  It indicates that the transformation to the correct form and content is incomplete.

As construction structure and construction materials are customized for this site, the notices in the material are changed to ones with green text.  These notices indicate which aspect of the material at that point and below is now in correct form: construction structure and content material.

For new material that is introduced following the initial bootstrap, a different green-text notice is used to indicate that this material is created in the proper form from the beginning. 

The different notices and their usage are illustrated here on the 2010-05-12 Announcement page.
