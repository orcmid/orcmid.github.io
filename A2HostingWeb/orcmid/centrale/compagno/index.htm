You've reached /centrale/compagno, a web collection of information about organization and operation of the Centrale Compagno computer.  Compagno is the Dell Inspiron laptop computer that is the portable heart of the Centrale system.  When docked on the Centrale network, Compagno remains a work horse and operates as a server to other Centrale computers.   When taken on the road, Compagno carries everything needed for local and distant operation on behalf of Centrale and for on-going mobile operation.
