/centrale/web

You've reached centrale/web, a section of Orcmid's Lair where everything about the Centrale computer complex is documented.  

Centrale is the complex of development systems that are used to construct and maintain all of the projects of NuovoDoc and the Orcmid's Lair business partnership and household.

Centrale is also distributed: the Web is used for coordination, both among local and remote systems and among hosting sites for the Centrale Web sites.  Because much of the Web structure is determined by tools and applications on local computers, and does not very by hosting site, there is documentation of Web experiences and approaches here.

This web page is part of the engineering, construction management, maintenance, and infrastructure of Orcmid's Lair.  You are welcome to explore this and other construction-information pages.   You'll need your hard hat and safety shoes.

To see a friendlier face for this material, go directly to the web section.   If that doesn't work, come back later.  The entrance is blocked off and detoured to here whenever the construction crew is blasting in the area.
