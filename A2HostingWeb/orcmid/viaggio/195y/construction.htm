This is the construction zone for the /viaggio /195y section.  This is the repository for image files that are published in some aspect of viaggio.  If images represent my own camera work, they are carried here using my long-time cataloging scheme, and then referenced from wherever they are used, including in Viaggio notes.

This section contains a structure for the 1950s images and their catalogs.  Each roll, numbered in chronological sequence, will have a subfolder within which appropriate images are retained along with notes, catalog information, and other notations.  The organization is not unlike how Blog posts are built in subdirectories, except these are for rolls of photographs instead of calendar months.  However, there is much material here that has no clear roll sequence and the year of the images may be unclear, hence 195y.

The development activities for this construction zone are recorded in the Construction Zone Diary & Job Jar.
