This folder provides Viaggio photographic images from roll F0412, taken from July 4 until processed on July 6, 2004.

July 4th, 2004 at my sister Judy's lake place (concluded)

  1. Rockets from an adjoining dock (no good fireworks)
  2. Like (1) but blurry and still no good fireworks
  3. Serious camera jitter viewing across the lake, but reasonably nice firework display in front of the trees
  4. Similar to (3) but with less jitter, not well focused
  5. Sister Carol Hamilton.  Very nice color and outlining against the dark.
  6. Blurrier picture across the lake - not useful
  7. Nice image over the lake with water reflection and good fireworks display
  8. Good dark shot of a display that sprays out well
  9. Same idea but not nearly as good as (8)
  10. Jittery picture of nice display across the lake -- it is finally dark enough
  11. Even more impressive than (10) and not so jittery
  12. Too jittery across the lake
  13. Wonderful shot across the lake - this is an amazing image of the spreading elements of a display
  14. Cut off at the top but also interesting for the reds and smoke
  15. Fountain display with jitter but pleasant result - would make a great narrow vertical strip
  16. More jitter and mildly interesting display
  17. On the dock with sparklers - no particular composition though
  18. Fireworks seen through tree branches - again, missing some composition
  19. Like (18) and interesting but not distinct
  20. Jittery but nice abstraction (these should all be gathered together on a black background)
  21. Another interesting abstraction (which way is up?)
  22. And yet another.  Very weirdly interesting patterns

    The Central Seattle Public Library (July 5 or 6, 2004)
     
  23. The central library of the Seattle Public Library system on what looks like the 5th avenue side, composition not too bad
