This folder provides Viaggio images that were gathered from other sources than standard photographic film.  These can be from other sites and from scanned material (but not negatives of my photographs).

  3. The Title and Authors heading of the May 1960 Report on the Algorithmic Language ALGOL 60.

  6. Sketch of Dennis Hamilton by Elizabeth Weeks Pankey made on March 14, 2006 at the Live Event of the inaugural Microsoft Small Business+ Summit, Bellevue Washington.
  7. Theme for the Microsoft Small Business+ Summit, March 14-17, 2006
