This folder provides Viaggio photographic images from roll F0618, begun on May 16, 2006, completed on June 6, and processed shortly thereafter.  Nikon 8008s with Sigma EX 105mm 1:2.8D Macro lense and Quantaray QTB 9500A electronic flash on side-mounted grip.  Film is Kodak Gold 200-6.

Brian Rice was also at MindCamp Seattle 2.0.  Brian has his own Lisp Machine.  I didn't know he was involved with SeaFunc, and was surprised to see him.  Apparently Conal learned about SeaFunc from Brian, and I learned about it from Conal.  I didn't learn, until hunting for Brian on the Internet, that our interests have surprising overlaps, especially in the area of logic, language, and computation, maybe even a little Heidegger.
