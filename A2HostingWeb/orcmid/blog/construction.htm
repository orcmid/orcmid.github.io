This is the Construction Zone for the Orcmid's Lair blog/ section.  There is a Construction Zone Diary & Job Jar for detailed work items that are part of developing material under this Zone.

Construction Zones typically use a diary and job jar as their only construction material.  If additional construction elements are introduced, they are catalogued on this page.

Although the content of this section is populated automatically using blog authoring-and-publishing software and services, there is a significant activity around creation, maintenance, and backup of the blog material.  This construction zone provides an account of that and any derived status information that is published in conjunction with the blog.
