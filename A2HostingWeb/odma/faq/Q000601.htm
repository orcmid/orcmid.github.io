1. Go to http://www.corel.com/support/newsgroup.htm for peer-to-peer newsgroups supported on the Corel News Server, cnews.corel.ca.
2. Subscribe to corelsupport.wordperfectoffice-2000.wordperfect_troubleshooting and find tips like this Deja.com message: Re: WPOffice2000 on Win2KPro vs. GroupWise
3. Search deja.news for ODMA.
4. Check on the Data Management Avenue list at http://documentmanagement.listbot.com
