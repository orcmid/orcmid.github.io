Immediate Content

This section holds the 00-block of template materials applicable in the construction of ODMA web pages and construction notes.  Templates and other special plumbing materials are provided in folios having identifiers of form C0000xx.  These are allocated in blocks for maintenance purposes. 

The blocks also provide a correspondence in depth of levels from the ODMA front porch that also matches the level of accession folders.  E.g., ODMA construction/templates/00/ matches the depth of ODMA construction/2006/01/, facilitating use of the templates for creation of content materials.

There are presently no folders below construction/templates/00/.

This construction section is part of the engineering, construction management, maintenance, and infrastructure for the ODMA Interoperability Exchange site.  You are welcome to explore this and other construction-information pages.   You'll need your hard hat and safety shoes.
