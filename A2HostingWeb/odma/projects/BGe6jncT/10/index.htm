This section holds embargoed ODMA project notes and related materials introduced in October, 2006.

This material is held back from the public odma/project structure and the catalog of projects until there is satisfaction that the material is ready for disclosure.  At that time, the folders will appear in the master catalog and and the material made available for widespread review and use.

All of the embargoed material is designed as it will appear when the embargo is lifted.  It is however, in a private location until that merger takes place.

There are no subordinate folders within the embargoed projects/2006/10.

The only content is material identified and linked from the catalog when the embargo is lifted.
