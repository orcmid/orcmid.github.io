ODMA Support

underway

 2000-04-06

Establish AIIM DMware ODMA Site
         

Formal management of ODMA support began in May 2000.  Incident reports, tips and responses to frequently-asked questions (FAQ), and project-tracking pages are added as formal support expands and matures.

Not all projects are conducted using this site.  The intention is that all public projects be located from this site, and that stable materials produced by projects will be available for download here.

New work on ODMA is generally carried out as part of ActiveODMA activity.  Those projects are also accounted for here, and there may be additional materials under the ActiveODMA area and its development project on SourceForge.
