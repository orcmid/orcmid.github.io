Forgive me if you've tried this already, but I have found from experience that the issue Microsoft documents in its Knowledge Base here http://support.microsoft.com/support/kb/articles/Q166/8/65.ASP?LN=EN-US&SD=gn&FR=0 also applies to Word 2000 & Windows 2000.

In essence Word tries to pass all strings as Unicode through ODMA & it must be instructed to use ANSI strings by creating a registry key

HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\ODMA32\ANSIonNT

I haven't tried out the sample DMS with Word, but I learnt the above from my experience with DOCS Open & SafetyGain, and the symptoms look similar.
