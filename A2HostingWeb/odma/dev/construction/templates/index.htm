This section holds template-material subfolders.  The templates are applicable to the construction of ODMdev web pages and construction notes.

For further information, refer to the low-numbered Template entries in

  • C000001: ODMdev Construction Materials Catalog

The catalog provides status and links to all ODMdev construction artifacts, including the available template materials.

Immediate Structure

00/

Initial block of templates.  The number on the folder determines the lowest-numbered material.  The numbers C000000 through C000099 are reserved for plumbing and templates.   The break to a new block of C0000xx folders can happen at any time.  The determining factor is maintenance convenience and nothing to do with the content.

This construction section is part of the engineering, construction management, maintenance, and infrastructure for ODMdev.  You are welcome to explore this and other construction-information pages.   You'll need your hard hat and safety shoes.
