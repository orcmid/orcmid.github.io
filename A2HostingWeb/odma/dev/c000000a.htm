The ODMA /dev folder is re-paved as ODMdev and operating as an independent, embedded Web site.  This allows maintenance to be managed separate from the overall ODMA site and leads toward havint ODMdev be independently mirrored, downloaded, recorded on CD-ROMs, etc.  The styles used for the site are modernized at the same time.  This qualifies as part of the ODMA Interoperability Exchange refresh.
0.00 2005-03-10-12:27 Initiation of Diary & Notes
This Construction Zone change history is set up for the ODMA /dev section working from sequestered to embargoed to public.  Note that private (that is, locked and requiring sign-on access) is a variant on public termed private.  An embargoed section could also be restricted, and we'll call that secret or confidential: some level where even the knowledge of the area is a secret.  This section is under the root of the ODMA.info site to shorten the URLs to interior content and also to resemble the kind of development tree implemented for com.orcmid.
