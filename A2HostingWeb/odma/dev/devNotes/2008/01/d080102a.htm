  2008-02-01 Review the related material list, making it complete and using the new titles being created as part of the ODMA Progression series.
  2008-02-01 I see the following key features: Smooth integration into application of the client: the GUI level and the application level.  Stability and dependability of integrations: this is especially important at clients, where breakdowns lead to serious inscrutable results as well as distrusting users.  This happens by addressing the application level of use, by never leaving the user stuck, providing coherence across all features that potentially touch on managed documents, and providing reliable troubleshooting of breakdowns where ODMA is somehow involved or suspected.  Finally, there is the matter of ease of entry for document-management integrations.  As a side issue, ODMA remains a niche arrangement.
