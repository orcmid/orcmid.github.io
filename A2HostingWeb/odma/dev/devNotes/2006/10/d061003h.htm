{Author Note: This is a placeholder used to sequester the 0.26alpha OdmNative100 integration.  It is produced for special testing and has no significant changes over the 0.20alpha OdmNative100 integration.  The Check05 test program is an adaptation of Setup04 that will be developed further for 0.30alpha.  The initial version here is a regression check to demonstrate that no defects were introduced in converting Setup04 and that no interface breakage resulted from internal modifications to OdmNative100  code.}
