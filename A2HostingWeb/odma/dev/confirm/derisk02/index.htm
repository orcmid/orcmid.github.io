You've reached the scaffolding for the ODMA Interoperability Exchange /dev/confirm/DeRisk02 subproject. 

This is the software-project folder for a risk-mitigation exercise conducted in the first steps of the ODMref 1.0 project.  This exercise is designed to quickly establish whether the necessary tools and development capability are in hand.

The time records for this activity are used to validate the estimation of effort for construction and testing of ODMref 1.0 and its companion test harness, ODMrun 1.0. 

  • DeRisk02 is a follow-on to DeRisk01.  The former was split into two tasks. The idea is to reduce the number of variations and experiments that are performed as one chunk.
  • The Main Feature of DeRisk02 is loading of a DLL.  This is the part of the experiment where we learn to include the proper headers and then create code for loading a DLL and calling its ODMGetODMInterface() entry.

This index.htm page is part of the engineering, construction management, maintenance, and infrastructure of the ODMA.info site.  You are welcome to explore this and other construction-information pages.   You'll need your hard hat and safety shoes.
