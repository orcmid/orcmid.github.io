Topics

  1. Characteristics of Electronic Document Management
    • Attention to document life cycle.
    • Situation of document in work process and business activity.
    • Metadata very important for context and for access of document.
    • Versioning and configurations of documents important 
  2. Relationship of Document Management and 
    • Records Management
    • COLD and Imaging Systems
    • Data Management
    • Content Management
  3. Convergence on Web Technologies
    •  HTML and Web Servers
    • XML
    • WebDAV
  4. Growing Demand for Records Management and Document Management
    • Archiving for business interruption
    • eGovernment use of electronic records in civil administration, law, work with suppliers and interaction with citizens
    • increased accountability: evidentiary use of electronic documents
    • healthcare and insurance systems reliance on electronic interchange of records
  5. No new document management standards work since ODMA and DMA.
  6. New standards work almost all related to Metadata and Internet/Web/XML.
    • Document Management Requirements not Satisfied
    • Recommended Practices and Additional Standards Needed
    • Common interest in Metadata Standards and in System Models where Document Management is a part
  7. AIIM Standards Activities
    • AIIM Standards Structure
    • ANSI Accreditation as Standards Development Organization
    • ISO TC 171 Document Imaging Subcommittees
    • Trend: Develop ISO Standards and adopt in USA

Discussion

Suggested topics for discussion include:

  • Where are the new standards and what is their status?
  • Importance of metadata registration, discovery, and document policies by standards or agreements on practice
  • Opportunities for exchange of information and cooperation on standards development in areas of common interest.
