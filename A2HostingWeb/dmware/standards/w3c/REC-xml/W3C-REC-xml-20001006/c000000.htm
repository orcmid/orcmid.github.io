Here's the running job jar of ideas and notes on the authentic creation of an annotated XML 1.0 specification.  

The annotation is for the purpose of situating XML and having direct relative linkage to passages of the XML 1.0 specification without having to leave the site or the mirror copy (file system, local-machine web server, or CD-ROM archive) of the annotated material.  The cross-references within the specification are replaced by shortcuts to labels in the same document.  

Access to the corresponding package of the official specification is by "purpling" the document.  Headings and other materials that are the targets of short cuts link to the identical passage in the official document for verification and confirmation.  In addition, there will be links from the annotated document to official errata and revisions, as they become known. 

Additional provisions will be documented here as they arise.
