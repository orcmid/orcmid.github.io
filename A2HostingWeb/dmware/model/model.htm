Status of the DMware Model

Current Draft

The last-posted DMware Model draft is 0.04.  This draft is posted to provide a pre-AIIM2000 checkpoint of all work to date.

Draft 0.05 is pending.  It will accompany posting of the Simple DMware license for widened discussion and review. 

History

  • (pending) Draft 0.05 will be the next version, accompanying review of the annotated license.
  • April 8, 2000: DMware draft 0.04 expands the reach of DMware to ODMA and other potential activities.  More simplification is begun around having a single, simple license available for all works.  And other open-source licenses can still be used by contributors who prefer them.
  • November 29, 1999: DMAware draft 0.03 responds to review comments by giving preference to a simple open-source license that does not require derivative works to be distributed under the same license.ĀSoftware under the simple license can be further developed as either open or closed source.
  • November 23, 1999.  DMAware draft 0.02 (Microsoft Word RTF) circulated with the ballot materials for dissolution of the Document Management Alliance.
  • A Microsoft Word RTF version of review draft 0.02 is available for download here.
  • November 10, 1999.  DMAware draft 0.01 (Microsoft Word RTF) circulated as part of discussion among DMA officers and AIIM for continuing DMA work via open-source projects.

Status of Simple DMware License

Current Draft

The last posted version of SDM 1.0 is draft 0.01.  This draft provides a pre-AIIM2000 checkpoint on current thinking.

Draft 0.02 is pending.  It will accompany posting of the DMware Model draft 0.05 for widened discussion and review. 

History

  • (pending).  Draft 0.02 will  be the next version, accompanying revision of the DMware Model.
  • February 7, 2000.  Draft 0.01 of the Simple DMware Open-Source License version 1.0 (annotated) is created with the essential structure and motivation information.
