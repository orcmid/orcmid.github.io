Change History

2003-03-24-16:57 Normalize section to use of standard "template" (orcmid)
1. The style and usage of FrontPage components is introduced to simplify future maintenance.
2. The activities are also brought under template control, with updated calendar information.
3. The section is ready for further updating of reference information on AIIM 2003.
 
2002-12-13-09:07 Establish Consistent Population of Activity Information (orcmid)
1. A Calendar and Past Events page are created.
2. The log for activities is updated for 2003, with placeholders for capture of retrospective information.

4. This material will provide a calendar and time-line framework for linking into collateral materials, such as FAQ, presentations, meeting minutes, and project activities.
5. There is minimal accommodation of cloaking conventions. 
   
2002-10-09-13:11 Create DMware Activities Section (orcmid)
The Activities section is created as a place to capture a record of activities already carried out and to also preserve material from the WEGO site that may disappear.
  1. The section is created by using the construction structure of the Projects section for boilerplate.
  2. A number of pages from the WEGO site are captured here as .mht files, for later review and preservation.
  3. This section is given a provisional index page in anticipation of later use as a general place to capture DMware activity-related information.
