Modular Metadata

Include France France presentation on Modular Metadata: Smaller "Nuts and Bolts" to Builder Larger, Wider Used Metadata.  (Thursday morning, Knowledge Management and Learning Technologies Track.)

His conclusions: Most metadata appears mappable to Modular Metadata.  Modular Metadata can be mapped to 11179-3.  There are these areas for further development: navigation, concept levels, UML primitives, 11404, SQL primitives, 11179-2 classification, 11179-4, 11179-5 naming conventions, 11179-6 registration, multilingual text, locales/contexts, and obligation.

Visit http://metadata-stds.org.  Follow the current work on 11179.  Can also subscribe to the e-mail distribution list.  E-mail Frank to get on that list.

Find his slide set and a way to link to it.  11179 is being modularized -- e.g., the contact information is being pulled out for separate use, security and others coming out as nuggets of the metamodel, and the metamodel incorporates them normatively.  There is apparently active work here, so 11179 is not fully stable.  Not clear how they are going to handle versioning of 11179 and the metamodel itself.  Maybe it is in there!  Farance says that some people will need to re-implement, but it should be mechanical.

Barriers to Adoption

  • Difference in business drivers and scopes
  • Seen as too easy then turns out to look too hard
  • Lowest common denominator is useless (who cares) - needs to address specialized needs that are experienced by a group
  • Tme and resource constraints - no long term plan or funding, must show immediate returns, this year and next 6 months - can have long range plan, but the near timeframe for results is critical
  • Difference in existing architectures and homebrew situations
  • Lack of understanding of gains and services available - appreciation for the outcome but not the process of getting there
  • Unwillingness to give up control 
  • Appeals to "good citizenship" don't work - there is more of a survival mentality
