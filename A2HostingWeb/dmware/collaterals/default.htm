AIIM Collaterals

DMware Collaterals are materials used in the creation of DMware materials, as well as documents and graphical items available for the promotion of DMware.  

Some of these materials are employed in the construction and maintenance of  DMware sections on the Web.  Others are used in the support of DMware activities and projects, such as creation of proposals, project definitions, and other materials used by DMware participants and contributors.

Images

DMware Images are illustrations incorporated in DMware materials:

The DMware Logo, in its standard size and format.
Also available in Microsoft Visio 2000 format.
 
The Hard Hat Logo, in its preferred size and format.
A derivative of a royalty-free Microsoft PowerPoint image, this image is employed on pages that are part of the construction structure of the DMware development site.

Templates

The DMware Templates collection includes templates for documents used as part of DMware activities.  These include templates for use with popular office products and for creating Web pages, including boiler plate documents and forms of various kinds.

The current set of templates is in the DMware Templates folder.
