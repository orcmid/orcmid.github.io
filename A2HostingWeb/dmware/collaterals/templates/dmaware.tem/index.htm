You've reached DMware/collaterals/templates/dmaware.tem, a Web-Development template designed for use with Microsoft FrontPage 98.

Although this section manages to capture the material under source-code management (Visual Source Safe) and preserve it on the DMware site, there are a number of concerns:

  • The material is obsolete and has not been maintained.
  • It is not known if the presence of this construction-structure information will interfere with the use of the material as a template.  

A more-appropriate way to make the template directory available would be in a Zip archive containing all of the material.  That change will be considered and then this location will be tombstoned in accordance with construction policies for the DMware development site.

For the moment this material is here to ensure connectivity of the site, even though this part is technically not a normal part of the DMware development web site, it just looks like it!
