1. WHAT ABOUT ARCHIVING SYSTEMS, REMOVABLE MEDIA, AND LEGACIES?
   ------------------------------------------------------------

When the jukebox wears out and the media is not compatible with any future technology, what is a boy to do?

There are no logical-data standards that provide for preservation of archival and long-term retention materials when media and managed collections are upgraded as technology changes faster than the lifetime of the materials.

Where are there links to any work in this area.  What kind of agreements made now will preserve investments in ultra-high capacity, long-retention, low-retrieval archive systems.  How is migration and conversion kept as automatic as possible -- no reindexing.
