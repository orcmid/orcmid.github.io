Do you have a question or concern?  Here's how to contact someone in the DMware community: 

  • Check the DMware-related Web Sites for information on particular areas of DMware.  There may be links and contacts specific to your concerns.  The web sites are useful for  locating the community that matches your interests.  Each DMware site has sections for  answers to Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ).

  • There are e-mail discussion lists that can be used to reach a particular community.  Find the list that works for you and submit a question, notice, or request.  Join a list to receive the discussion via e-mail.

  • Stuck?  You have something different to contact us about?  Please send e-mail to one of the administrative contacts.

Web Sites

AIIM International.  Go to http://www.aiim.org.  Click "Standards" on the Community and Education menu.

DMware Development Site.  You're here in a mirror of the DMware Development site.  The official Web URL is http://DMware.info.  This site and related sites have FAQ sections with further information:

  • DMA: Document Management Alliance FAQ
  • DMware: General DMware FAQ
  • ODMA: Open Document Management API FAQ

The FAQ pages provide further links and avenues to contacts and support.

Discussion Lists

E-mail discussion lists are the recommended means for reaching experts that are paying attention to particular areas of DMware.  There is information on how to review discussions, submit messages, and subscribe to the DMware-related discussion lists:

  • DMA: Using DMA Tech
  • ODMA: Using ODMA Tech
  • WebDAV: Using the WebDAV Discussion List

There may be additional specialized discussion lists beyond the general ones listed.  Consult the individual sites for details.
