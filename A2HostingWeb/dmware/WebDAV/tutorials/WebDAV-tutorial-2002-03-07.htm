WebDAV Tutorial (March 7, 2002)

What is it and how do I make it work?

This tutorial, demonstration, and discussion features existing, already-available provisions of Microsoft Office, Web Folders, and WebDAV repositories. We'll explore what you may not know you already have available for developing and practicing with document-management using web technologies.

What does it take to establish web-based document management?

Follow-On Discussion (March 8, 2002)

The WebDAV tutorial identifies readily-available fixtures for simple introduction of web-based document management on the standard office desktop.

What else is needed to qualify and establish managed web-document practices?

What is the gap between components of generic technology and what it takes to operate smoothly with managed documents in practical office settings?

We're after a road map for pulling together available information, identifying what else is needed, and exploring ways of obtaining it through community effort. Our target is a practical guide to successful, affordable practices.
