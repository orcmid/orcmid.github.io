Sketch

Startup for RTFX development involves

  • Identification and capture of specifications and of essential materials as part of the RTFX Development Resources.
  • Search for available tools for parsing of RTF and for patching RTF files.
  • Proof-of-concept demonstration of packaging and launching applications on extracted RTF files.
  • Provision of minimal repairs based on introduction of explicit character set and code-page dependencies of RTF files using single-byte character encodings.
  • Confirming a more stream-lined automatic launching of portable fix-ups of the preserved RTF.
  • Development of base requirements and use cases as a foundation for progressive refinement and demonstrated/tested achievement of RTFX functionality and usability.

The next stages involve

  1. setup of a tool-chain for cross-platform production of RTFX fixtures and concept demonstrations
  2. automation of launching on RTFX files that fires up a silent extraction and relaunching tool of an RTF-accepting processor as an available association
  3. command-line, console level creation of RTFX packages and a means for progressive addition of fix-up capability.

The work is personal and voluntary and their is no promise of sustained effort on any road map.  There are shared-prerequisite efforts that will gate progress as well.

Assumptions

  • RTFX format uses a Document Container File adaptation of the ODF 1.2 Package specification.  This is a custom use of the ODF 1.2 Package, along the same lines as under PCT.
      
  • RTFX documents have an ODF cover that identifies what they are and discourages attempt to use as ODF documents.
     
  • The extraction of a document with any hot fix-ups is into a read-only fixed temporary-file location.
      
  • Launching of an extracted temporary as read-only should prevent saving over the transient extraction, so that users will not be mislead into assuming that a Save can modify the RTFX form.
      
  • Having a GUI client for reviewing and making what-if fixes to RTF parameters is a long-term goal but there must be very early means for capturing low-hanging fruit and evolving whatever the processing model must be for representing fix-up conditions, eligible launch applications, etc.  Down-level consistency will be important very early, but not necessarily in start-up.
      
  • There is always something that is working, at whatever crude level.
