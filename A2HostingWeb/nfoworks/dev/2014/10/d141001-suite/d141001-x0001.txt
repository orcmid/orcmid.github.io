           ODF CHANGE-TRACKING LIST CROSS-CUT DEFECT d141001-x0001
           =======================================================

                     LIBREOFFICE DEFECT DEMONSTRATION

    The defect in this report is confirmed in Libreoffice 4.3.1.2 and has
    appeared at least as far back as OpenOffice.org 2.4.3.

    This is a Works-for-Me-Not defect.  The tracked change shown to the
    user is not how the tracked-change is saved in the resulting file,
    losing the change as it was observed when it was made.

    The latest archive covering this defect case is available on the
    Internet at <http://nfoWorks.org/dev/2014/10/d141001-suite#x0001>.

d141001-x0001.txt is this manifest and defect demonstration
