The Original Lidded Pot

Vicki created the lidded pot in July, 1983, at a pottery workshop with Warren MacKenzie at Big Creek Pottery near Santa Cruz, California.

The Lidded-Pot Image

The lidded pot was the inspiration and model for the image drawn by Vicki using the Windows 98 version of Microsoft Paint.  It is used on stationery, business cards, signs, and now as the theme symbol for Millennia Antica on the Web.

New Lidded-Pot Series

Lidded pots based on the original theme are created newly from time to time.  There are recent versions in the studio and on sale.
