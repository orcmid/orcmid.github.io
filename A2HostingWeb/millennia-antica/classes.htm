Call Burien Parks & Recreation Department at 206-988-3700 to register for all classes or go on-line to the City of Burien, Washington. 

 

Vicki's  2013 Class Schedule

Fall Quarter, September 5 - November 25

Pottery Wheel
September 9 - November 25, 2013
Monday Evenings 6:30 - 9pm - 11 classes
     No class November 11
Class Fee:  $246 (Burien Residents $235)
                  includes glazes and firing
Ages:  15 and up
This class is for students who have learned the fundamentals of throwing.  Work with the instructor to increase your skill level in throwing as well as learn more about glazing and firing.  It is recommended that students take Beginning Pottery Wheel before signing up for this class.

Beginning to Intermediate Pottery Wheel
September 12 - November 21, 2013
Thursday evenings 6:30-9pm - 11 classes
Class Fee:  $246 (Burien Residents $235)
                  includes glazes and firing
Ages:  15 and up
This class is for people new to wheel throwing or those who have learned the basics and want to take their work to the next level.  Fundamentals of centering, throwing, trimming and glazing will be covered in this course.  For those with more experience, we will explore altered forms, textured surfaces, and use of clay slip as a decorative element.

Be prepared to set down your day, relax and open yourself to a whole new creative experience!
