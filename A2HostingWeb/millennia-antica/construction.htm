2001-12-30-12:00 Set up Web Forwarding of Millennia-Antica.com (orcmid)
1. The domain aliasing provided by bCentral is only good for aliasing the root domain name.
2. The Interland FAQ provided information about companies that provide domain forwarding, URL masking, and also redirecting entries at the root.  I didn't like the flavor of any of these services.
3. I discovered that Network Solutions provides web forwarding for essentially $1 per month.  So I set up forwarding of Millennia-Antica.com to nfoCentrale.net/millennia-antica/ and it is operating.
4. This means that the target domain name will be visible and will apply when pages are bookmarked or put into favorites.  I am now looking at a solution for that.  It may be by some device to implement in the Millennia Antica subweb itself.  This seems like a desirable solution.  No matter how one gets into a subweb on the site, the URL that is provided would always reflect the particular domain being mirrored.  I need to deal with preserving independence of mirror-site pages from their anchor-site location, in that case.
 
2001-12-29-13:21 Provisional Structure for Millennia Antica on bCentral (orcmid)
1. A miniature Millennia Antica site is constructed using the Centrale compagno laptop's PWS, FrontPage 2000, and Visual Source Safe 6.0.
2. The three initial sections and the root are given construction-structure pages and an entrance default page is defined.
3. The idea is to confirm establishment of Millennia-Antica.com as a sub-domain of the nfoCentrale.net site hosted by Microsoft bCentral.  The initial material is complete, with no broken links.  Once the provisional structure is established, meaningful content can be introduced by progressive refinements.
4. Beside providing the first appearance of Millennia Antica on the web, the provisional material is designed to explore two capabilities on bCentral: hosting of additional domains (i.e., millennia-antica.com) and the consequences of relative links to anchor-site locations (i.e., nfocentrale.net/construction/).
5. 2001-12-30: I confirmed that bCentral's domain aliasing won't do what I want.  Instead, Web forwarding is available, and it doesn't mask the nfoCentrale.net URL.  So relative links work just fine, because all URLs are for the anchor site.
