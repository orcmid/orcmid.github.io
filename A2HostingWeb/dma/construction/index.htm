You've reached DMA /construction , a part of the support site for AIIM's DMware DMA Interoperability Exchange.  This area is devoted to information about the construction and maintenance of DMA material.

The construction section is part of the engineering, construction management, maintenance, and infrastructure for DMAtech.info.  You are welcome to explore this and other construction-information pages.   You'll need your hard hat and safety shoes.

If you'd rather find out about DMA instead of admire the scaffolding, organization, and implementation of the web site, go directly to the official DMA mirror site or to the overall DMware development site.   If that is unsuccessful, return later.  The DMA site is often blocked off while the construction crew is working here.
