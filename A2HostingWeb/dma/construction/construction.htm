2002-12-10-08:59 Create Initial DMA/construction section (orcmid)
   1. In order to allow independent mirroring of the  DMAtech.info sections, the nfoCentrale.net construction structure is introduced.  This structure supports (mostly) single-source maintenance that supports multiple locations of mirrors (e.g., on CD-ROM, at a different web location, or on a hard drive directory structure).  
   2. This construction section and a _private section are cloned from the same sections of the ODMA.info mirror. 
   3. Some initial practices for minimal accommodation of cloaking are also begun with the introduction of consistent construction structure.  The construction structure pages always link to other construction-structure pages by "true" URL.  The other pages apply minimal-accommodation rules.  The basic idea is that construction structure is always for a given physical site, and not any cloaked "virtual" site.
