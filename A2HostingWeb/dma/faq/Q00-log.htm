The History of ODMA

Formal management of DMA support began in May 2000.  Tips and responses to frequently-asked questions (FAQ) are added as found to be valuable in responding to questions and in troubleshooting.  DMA is inactive and the present FAQ is intended to support archiving of the site and DMA historical information in a complete form.

As capture of material under AIIM DMware continues, retroactive items with earlier numbers are also added.  These items preserve useful topics that were addressed informally before.
