You've reached www.infonuovo.com/dma/cbsearch , a collection of information about the Content-Based Search Extensions to the DMA 1.0 Specification.  This is where you can find out

  • what is CBSearch,
  • how it fits with DMA 1.0,
  • how CBSearch is used to provide interoperability among compound-structured documents systems,
  • the benefits of CBSearch,
  • how CBSearch fits in the overall scheme of things and how it is used to enable interoperability among DMA document collections and above legacy document systems
  • sources of more information
