DMA

The Document Management Alliance
Last updated 2022-10-10T20:05Z

The AIIM Document Management Alliance (DMA) specifications provide a model and API for interoperable integration of document-management repositories.  Focus is on distributed plug-and-play integration into enterprise solutions. 

NOTICE: The DMAtech.info domain has been retired.  The original site pages are preserved here at https://nfoCentrale.com/dma/ for historical purposes.  Support and maintenance are no longer available.
