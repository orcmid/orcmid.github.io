webDMA

Web-Enabled DMA

The proposed webDMA project of the DMA Technical Committee establishes web-enabled operation for DMA-based document management systems.  The integration of DMA and the Web is standards-based: access to DMA facilities is via HTTP, WebDAV, and XML, with an appropriate mapping of concepts between the two models.
