You've reached www.infonuovo.com/dma/csdocs/relmodel , a collection of information about Compound-Structured Documents Extensions involving the DMA 1.0 Relationship Model.

  • Relationships are fundamental to the CSDocs Foundation, as addressed with the introduction of CSRelationships as part of the CSDocs Foundation.
  • Relationships also figure into the introduction of Virtual Elements and their explicit reflection in VERelationship objects.
  • Beyond applications of the DMA 1.0 Relationship Model, there are also extensions to the relationship model for navigation (in CSRelationships) and in the use of Relationship objects to support referential integrity of connections among different persistent objects of a DMA Document Space.

The first CSDocs proposal that amends the DMA 1.0 Relationship Model is the proposal for explicitly controlling relationship strength.

This and other sections of infonuovo.com/dma provide an unofficial guide to the DMA Technical Committee effort on the DMA 1.0 specification, proposed extensions, and trial-use recommendations.
