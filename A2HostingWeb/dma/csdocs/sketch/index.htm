You've reached www.infonuovo.com/dma/csdocs/sketch , the location for a sketch of the Compound-Structured Documents Extensions to the DMA 1.0 Specification:

  • what is CSDocs,
  • how it fits with DMA 1.0,
  • how CSDocs is used to provide interoperability among compound-structured documents systems,
  • the benefits of CSDocs,
  • how CSDocs fits in the overall scheme of things and considerations for introduction in an interoperable way with other standards and other DMA implementations
  • sources of more information

This is not exactly the definitive source of information about CSDocs.   The latest information on this sketch, until it is approved as a supplement to DMA 1.0, is in the record of the DMA CSDocs Subcommittee.  That material has not been added here yet.

This web page, www.infonuovo.com/dma/csdocs/sketch/index.htm, is part of the engineering, construction management, maintenance, and infrastructure of the InfoNuovo web.  You are welcome to explore this and other construction-information pages.   You'll need your hard hat and safety shoes.

If you'd rather find out about DMA's approach to Compound-Structured Documents than admire the scaffolding, organization, and implementation of the infonuovo.com site, go directly to the DMA/CSDocs/sketch web folder.   If that doesn't work, come back later.  The folder is blocked off whenever the construction crew is working here.
