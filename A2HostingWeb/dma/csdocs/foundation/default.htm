Download Locations

You are viewing a possible unofficial mirror of the CSDocs Foundation material.  It is not necessarily current or accurate.

To obtain the latest version, look in two places: ftp://aiim.org and http://www.infonuovo.com/dma/csdocs/foundation.

  • The AIIM DMA FTP Site's ftp://ftp.aiim.org/DMA/TechnicalCommittee/CSDocs/Proposals subdirectories are the definitive locations for current proposals by the DMA CSDocs Subcommittee.  The Foundation proposals have names like CSDF003.ZIP, where the numerals are related to the version of the particular edition.  Download the one with the most-recent version in its name from the New or Approved subdirectory.

    ftp.aiim.org is a moderated site.  You will need an User ID and a Password to access the material.  All members of DMA have access.   If you don't know the password, request it from one of the officers of the DMA Technical Committee.

  • The InfoNuovo.com site location http://www.infonuovo.com/dma/csdocs/foundation.   A recent version of the material is at that location in the form of viewable web pages.  This is provided as a convenience for DMA Tech readers who may find it difficult to access ftp.aiim.org.  This is not guaranteed to be the definitive source of the information.  The latest, official version is always on ftp.aiim.org.
