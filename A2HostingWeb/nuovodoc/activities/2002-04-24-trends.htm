Difficulty of operating without transaction system, what to do about that in mission-critical, high-reliability systems.  Recommendation is that there is no practical way to work-around or to have a simulation of transactioning.   Must be used where scenarios allow for safe failure modes and safety in the face of incomplete or partial operations.
