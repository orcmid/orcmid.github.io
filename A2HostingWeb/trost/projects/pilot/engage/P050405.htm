Objective: 
Provide the information necessary for someone to become familiar with the SourceForge TROST project and choose whether to observe, experiment, test, document, or participate in active development and customization of the TROST framework.
1. Have the SourceForge site customized enough for initial use and exploration.
2. Have customization and supplemental information so that those unfamiliar with the SourceForge toolcraft and tacit assumptions can find what they need to operate successfully.
3. Demonstrate activity on the SourceForge site.
4. Expand to support developer activity when there is anything developed to share.

Conditions of Satisfaction:
1. Sufficient Material Posted on SourceForge and TROSTing for people to be able to engage on the SourceForge site.
2. A sufficient initial structure on how to find and use the available resources
3. Materials usable in regularly referring others to the project.
