The glossary is being compiled now as terms arise and definitions are thought to be warranted.  Sometimes it is because we want to clarify the specific usage of familiar terms as they are applied here (e.g., trust and trustworthiness).  We also define technical terms that are specific to information technology and software development (e.g., bootstrap and API).

The glossary is meant to be used as a hypertext with links to expanded information and providing as linked-to explanations of terms that can be referenced in other InfoNotes and articles.

The current glossary page is a catch-all for terms as they are noticed.  Many of the terms lack definitions, and there is little connected explanation for those that have definition entries.  This will change as TROST documentation evolves.
