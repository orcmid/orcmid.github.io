NGC 5426/27 photo credit: Vera C. Rubin (1977)

The image of galaxies in collision provides a metaphor for

  • different (spiraling) lifecycles levels in the creation and adoption of computer and information systems
  • distinct conceptual frameworks inhabited by practitioners in their separate universes of practice
  • bridging the gap, with new understandings and opportunities that arise in the fusion
