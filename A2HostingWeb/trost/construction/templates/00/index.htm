Immediate Content

This section holds the 00-block of template materials applicable in the construction of TROSTing web pages and construction notes.  Templates and other special plumbing materials are provided in folios having identifiers of form c0000xx.  These are allocated in blocks for maintenance purposes. 

The blocks also provide a correspondence in depth from the TROSTing front porch that also matches the level of accession folders.  E.g., TROSTing construction/templates/00/ matches the depth of  TROSTing construction/2011/11/, dev/2006/07/, etc., facilitating use of the templates and their construction structures as boilerplate for creation of content materials.

There are presently no folders below templates/00.
