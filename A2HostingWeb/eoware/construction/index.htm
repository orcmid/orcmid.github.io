You've reached the eoWare Construction Office and Hard Hat Area. Here's where there is information on

  • what the eoWare.com web site is and where is it located
  • how the site is constructed and managed
  • the construction history of the site, reflected in the interconnected construction zones
  • what problems are being worked on
  • what is coming up for the site
  • what resources are gathered for support of site construction

This web page, /construction/index.htm , is part of the engineering, construction management, maintenance, and infrastructure of the nfoWare site.  You are welcome to explore this and other construction-information pages.   You'll need your hard hat and safety shoes.

Every section (mini-Web) of the nfoWare site has an index.htm page.  These pages accomplish three things:

  • ensure that the index (the web-site directory) of every eoWare section is hidden and the only access to material of the site is via navigation or use of a known, published URL
  • provide a default page for entry to areas when a default.htm (or default.asp) page is missing during site construction and updates.
  • tie together the construction structure for the entire nfoWare site.

For example, if you reach this page when navigating to construction/, you know that this section of the web site is itself under construction and any other default page is missing (or non-existent).  You'll have to return later to see if this section of the site ever provides anything more than its own Hard Hat area (a.k.a Construction Zone).
