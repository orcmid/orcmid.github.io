As part of setting up the bootspiral for nfoWiki, I want to make sure that I design a clean WikiText to use in the initial round.  This can be provisional and the implementation can be loose, but certain steps around versioning, identification, and honoring of prevalent practice, would be nice to incorporate and anticipate.  Here are the WikiText Conventions that I have identified to guide my initial steps.

Convention

Significance

 Used By
 lines of plain text take as (marked-up) content MediaWiki
blank (empty) line separates paragraphs MediaWiki
white space within paragraphs, treated the same as XML white-space MediaWiki
<br> breaks lines without starting a new paragraph MediaWiki
