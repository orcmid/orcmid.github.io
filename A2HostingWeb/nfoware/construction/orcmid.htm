Orcmid
is a pseudonym (or "nom de bit" or "pseudonimo della Rete") of Dennis E. Hamilton, NuovoDoc System Architect.  Dennis is the web master for nfoCentrale.net as well as its predecessor, InfoNuovo.com.

Dennis conceived of nfoWare as a companion to work being done in document management and document processing and reflected on DMware.  More-recently, nfoWare is seen as an opportunity to develop resources and an approach to "information" processing that is based on ideas of situated computation and situated data.  This site is being established to carry an expansion on those topics.
