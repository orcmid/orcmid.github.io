You've reached the Strumenti section of nfoCentrale. Here's where there is information on the software, hardware, and components of the nfoCentrale complex.

This collection was originally established on Orcmid's Lair.  It is resuscitated on this nfoCentrale anchor site because of the wide applicability and value in coordinated management of work items and use of tools across the complex.

I chose gli strumenti (instruments) instead of gli attrezzi (tools or equipment) because I have already used initial A for other purposes in my cataloging scheme.  There's nothing more interesting to it than that, beyond the challenge to find Italian words for some related purposes.

This web page, index.htm, is part of the engineering, construction management, maintenance, and infrastructure of  the nfoCentrale complex.  It is placed here, as in every section of the site, as a placeholder, initial scaffolding, and temporary "home page" while the section is under construction.  The Construction Structure is a skeletal underlay throughout all sites and is never removed.  

You are welcome to explore this and other construction-information pages.   You'll need your hard hat and safety shoes.
