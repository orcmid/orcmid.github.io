20 Active nfoCentrale Domain Names

By Dennis E. Hamilton on September 1, 2010 11:52 PM | No Comments | 3 TrackBacks

[update 2010-09-02T20:07Z I added the orcmid.tv category and tidied up the table a little bit.  I am also noticing the many ways that Windows Live Writer fails to handle editing of more-intricate [X]HTML content.  After all of this time in the history of HTML editors, I am not clear what is so difficult about this, apart from misplaced NIH ego-foo.]

I have 20 domain names all to be hosted at nfoCentrale.  The sites category provides subcategories on their individual development and status.

There are also two retired domain names.  I’ve commented on the demise of InfoNuovo already.  Until I sat down to create this list and check on all of the current leases, I had forgotten about the never-used orcmid.tv.  It keeps infonuovo.com company in the list of retirees, below.
