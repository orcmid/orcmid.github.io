This section holds nfoCentrale Status images/ material for 2010.  The images are mainly loaded by use of tools that make direct FTP transfer of the images as part of blog-page publishing.  If there is excessive material for accumulation in the course of a full year, further subdivision will be made in some way.

Because the tools create specially-generated folder and image names, no effort is made to manage those.  Instead, along with backup to a hosted-site image on a development server, I also create an images.zip archive of the material as an additional backup.  I usually have the origiinal images somewhere else already, but the images.zip reflects exactly how the material was organized and formatted on the hosted site for use from blog posts.  The images.zip is also versioned in VSS and it is shared to the development web, as additional backup.

In any case, this material is not meant to be reached by deep-linking or direct navigation.  There is no assurance that link integrity will be preserved.
