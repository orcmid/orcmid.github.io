Immediate Content

This section holds the 00-block of template materials applicable in the construction of nfoCentrale anchor web pages and construction notes.  Templates and other special plumbing materials are provided in folios having identifiers of form c0000xx.  These are allocated in blocks for maintenance purposes. 

The blocks also provide a correspondence in depth from the nfoCentrale front porch that also matches the level of accession folders.  E.g., nfoCentrale construction/templates/00/ matches the depth of  nfoCentrale construction/2006/01/, facilitating use of the templates for creation of content materials.

There are presently no folders below templates/00.  The templates/10/ folder commences with c000010, etc.
